UMS magazines on board Oman Air

The publications of United Media Service (UMS), the largest media establishment in Oman, will now be available on board Oman Air, the national carrier of the Sultanate of Oman. Oman Air passengers traveling on long-haul Airbus A330s can now read the digital edition of all the business, lifestyle and luxury magazines of UMS, both in Arabic and English on the aircraft’s In-Flight Entertainment System. The UMS magazines displayed on board the Oman Air flights include Oman Economic Review (OER), and Alam al-Iktisaad Wal A’mal (AIWA), Oman’s premium business magazines respectively in English and Arabic, The Woman, Al Mar’a, and Signature.

Job Opportunities

  


 Who we are

UMS (United Media Services SAOC), a Middle East-based media, marketing and publishing organisation, providing creative and strategic solutions derived from our inherent understanding of the region’s markets, people, branding, business, culture, aesthetics, and media. We are currently strengthening our teams in Muscat and Qatar and can offer you exciting opportunities.

 Who you should be

Assistant Editor: A talented and highly motivated individual to assist with bringing out our leading monthly business magazine as well as handle specific projects, individually as well as collectively, undertaken through our regional network. Your task will also include planning, writing and providing editorial requirements for our other local as well as international titles. You should have excellent communication, interpersonal and editorial skills, a great track record of producing and editing articles and news stories for magazines or newspapers, as well as significant experience in news management. We want to hear from journalists who are fully-trained in both QuarkXPress and Microsoft Word and with at least eight years’ experience.

Bureau Chief: A top-notch, seasoned, resourceful and creative print journalist capable of managing reporting and editing assignments for our general-interest magazine as well as contributing ideas and expertise for our other titles. Your task will also include assisting the editor in supervising a staff of talented reporters and editors, help oversee publications, and be responsible for the smooth and economical operation of the bureau. You should possess knowledgeability in a wide range of subject areas, a substantial word processing experience and a great track record of producing articles and news stories for magazines or newspapers, with at least six years of experience in media vehicles of repute. Experience of QuarkXpress will be an advantage though not a prerequisite for qualification.

Feature writer-cum-sub editor: An ambitious and self-motivated editorial resource with a sharp eye for detail and a burning desire to learn and prove oneself in any task assigned by the editor. Your task will include evaluating and aligning the writers’ contributions to our overall media objectives by making appropriate adjustments besides providing reporting support for the general-interest magazine as well as our other titles. We will expect the right candidate to be an outstanding sub (fabulously witty and pithy headline/standfirst/caption writing is a must, as is an eagle-eye for proofs). You should have a reasonable track record of producing articles and news stories for magazines or newspapers, with at least two years of experience. Experience of QuarkXpress will be an advantage though not a prerequisite for qualification.

Designer: An individual with exceptional design ability to manage the design and production of our general-interest and lifestyle magazines. You should be able to conceptualise, visualize and design. The successful candidate will have a minimum of four years’ experience in publication and advertising design. You must be extremely responsible, creative, and a fast designer but attentive to detail and accurate in your execution. Knowledge of the Macintosh platform, QuarkXpress, Photoshop and Illustrator is a must.

Advertising Media Executives: An ambitious go-getter with innovative space selling ideas. You should have a minimum of 2 years of experience in magazine advertising sales, good presentation skills and brand ownership. You should have an excellent track record of successful sales performance. Willingness to work hard, positive attitude and a “Can Do” approach are some of the prerequisites.

Sales Executives for interactive business: You should have a minimum of 2 years of experience in marketing web and interactive services. Sound knowledge of ICT and an excellent track record of successful sales performance.

You will agree that going ‘green’ is not just a buzz word but indeed a very human issue that transcends companies and markets to touch us all - It is about time that we all look at how we can contribute to safeguarding our environment for our present, and for future generations.

Oman Economic Review has started a movement to champion those organisations and individuals that are making a difference to Oman’s environment. We have created the Oman Green Awards as a platform from which green initiatives can be showcased, to inspire and galvanize others for the benefit of our environment locally, regionally and globally.

The full weight of our media vehicles will be aligned with the purpose of continually advocating local initiatives that make a difference, popularizing achievements, and creating a far-reaching network that links us together in this common cause. With the Awards held annually we are creating a focal point to the endeavor; an annual goal to keep us striving year on year to enrich the connection and further push all stakeholders to get involved and help build the platform as an authoritative meeting point for all those that are committed to Oman’s environment.

Oman Green Awards is our contribution to the cause; but we acknowledge that there has to be collective effort and active participation of Oman’s business community to sustain and make it a national success.

We invite you to join us as a Green Oman Partner and Inspire a better tomorrow.

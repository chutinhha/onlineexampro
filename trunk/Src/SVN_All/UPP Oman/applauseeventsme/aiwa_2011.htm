ALAM AL-IKTISAAD WAL A'MAL AWARDS FOR OMAN’S BEST PERFORMING COMPANIES 2011

Alam al-Iktisaad Wal A’mal (AIWA), Oman’s leading Arabic monthly business magazine, brings to light new insights and forecasts on future trends that impact Oman’s corporate world, making it a valuable source for decision making and an indispensable tool for advertisers and marketers in Oman.

EVENT OVERVIEW
To recognize the top achievers in Oman’s corporate world, AIWA has instituted Awards for Oman's Best Performing Companies. In a red carpet awards evening at Shangri-La Barr Al Jissah on 27 June this year, AIWA felicitated the top performing listed companies in Oman during 2010.

A networker’s dream, the event was attended by the who’s who of Muscat corporate high life. The event attracted more than 300 business leaders, top managers and senior government officials from a cross-section of domains.

OMAN’S BEST PERFORMING COMPANIES
Overall 15 Top Ranking Companies received the coveted trophy. There were five winners in each of the following three categories:

Large-Cap (market-cap above RO75 million)

  • AHLIBANK SAOG
  • OMAN QATARI TELECOMMUNICATION SAOG
  • OMAN OIL MARKETING COMPANY SAOG
  • BANKDHOFAR SAOG
  • SHELL OMAN MARKETING COMPANY SAOG

Mid-Cap (market-cap between RO25 million to RO75 million)

  • AL ANWAR CERAMIC TILES COMPANY SAOG
  • MUSCAT FINANCE COMPANY LTD. SAOG
  • OMAN REFRESHMENT COMPANYS SAOG
  • A’ SAFFA FOODS SAOG
  • AL OMANIYA FINANCIAL SERVICES SAOG

Small-Cap (market cap between RO10 million to RO25 million)

  • OMAN CHROMITE COMPANY SAOG
  • MUSCAT GASES COMPANY SAOG
  • OMAN FIBER OPTIC COMPANY SAOG
  • OMAN CHLORINE SAOG
  • SAHARA HOSPITALITY CO. SAOG

The winners were selected based on their performance on the five key financial parameters including Revenue Growth, Net Profit Growth, Return on Average Assets, Return on Average Equity and Net Profit Margin. The ranking process wais done by Gulf Baader Capital Markets (GBCM). The ranking results were validated by KPMG.

EXCELLENCE IN CORPORATE LEADERSHIP
AIWA also felicitated two corporates with ‘Award for Excellence in Corporate Leadership’ for not only reaching the pinnacle of corporate success but also making an immense contribution in the social upliftment with their unparalleled actions. The winning companies were:

  • Oman Telecommunications Company (Omantel)
  • BankMuscat

PERSONALITY AWARDS
Alam al-Iktisaad Wal A’mal felicitated two prominent Omani nationals who have done the nation proud by excelling in the fields of business and economy. The awards were presented in the following two categories:

GLOBAL OMANI OF THE YEAR AWARD
Dr. Mohammed Al-Barwani, the Chairman of MB Holding Company

LIFETIME ACHIEVEMENT AWARD
Sheikh Suhail Salim Bahwan, the Chairman of Suhail Bahwan Group

Session I – Panel Debate
Subject: Building National Talent – Achievements So Far, Gaps and Related Challenges.

Panel discussion on the current status of development of national talent, gap between the demand and supply of talent, and the associated challenges. The panelists will focus on :
• The success in the development of national talent
• The gap between the demand and supply of skilled manpower
• Key industries and functional areas that are still reliant heavily on expatriate workforce
• Major challenges in building national talent especially education system, manpower planning, professional training, etc.
• Lack of public-private partnerships

Session II – Panel Debate
Subject: Solutions to the Challenges & the Way Forward.

Panel discussion on what are the possible solutions to meet the talent gap and develop Omani talent on a sustainable basis to support the national growth engine. The panel will debate on:
• Role of the Government in meeting the challenge of developing the national talent pool through policy frameworks and infrastructure growth
• How private sector can contribute in creating a strong human capital
• Effective public-private partnerships
• How civil society can make the difference in creating more awareness about the need for self-reliance, higher education and vocational training.

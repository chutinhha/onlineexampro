Be a part of OER Corporate Challenge 2007!

 

1.

To participate in OER Corporate Challenge 2007, all you need to do is form a team of eight corporate warriors representing your organisation.

2.

Ideally, each team should comprise 3 players each from senior and middle management, plus 2 players at an executive level.

3.

All team members need to be medically fit to take part in OER Corporate Challenge 2007.

4.

Participation Fee for OER Corporate Challenge is RO 500. (Covers Tournament Kit and 2-day hospitality for participants.)

5.

Participation in OER Corporate Challenge 2007 is limited to only 30 corporate teams.

6.

A random selection of teams will take place. On confirmation of your entry, you will be sent an event registration form and other details.

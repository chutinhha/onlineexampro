Corporate World Gears up for Forthcoming Challenge
Times of Oman - October 21, 2006

MUSCAT: The corporate world of Muscat is abuzz with excitement and anticipation as they look forward to the OER Corporate Challenge 2006 to be held on 29th and 30th November at the exotic Oman Dive Centre. After the resounding success of the OER CEO Golf 2006, OER is geared to set new standards of camaraderie and team spirit by way of games designed to challenge the body and mind of the participants, representing leading business houses and corporations of Muscat.

The two day mega event will bring together top decision makers of the corporate sector along with their key company representatives to engage in healthy competition, alongside staunch network and relationship building with the key players in the Oman business market in an entertaining and fun filled environment. For the first time in Oman, the corporate teams will contest for the prestigious Champions trophy which will be felicitated to the winning team, followed by the Arabian night theme and the gala dinner.

The event has clearly captured the attention of the corporate world and of the general public so much so that the event is expected to attract spectator strength of over 1000 people over two days. Companies like OMAN MOBILE, JEEP, TIMES OF OMAN & SHABIBA, AXA INSURANCE, OMAN PRINTERS & STATIONERS LLC, OUA & CAPITAL STORE have associated with us as partners and supporting sponsors.

The OER Corporate Challenge is yet another pioneering initiative by the Oman Economic Review (OER) launched in 1998 with an aim to bridge the business gap in the Sultanate. The OER today is by far the leading business publication in Oman, consistently maintaining high standards of excellence over the years, OER has become a must read for decision makers who value reliable, timely and authoritative business news coverage.

7 March, 2012

After Compliments,

We are pleased to inform you that under the patronage of Ministry of Manpower, United Media Services LLC (UMS) is organizing OMAN FORUM–BUILDING NATIONAL TALENT on Tuesday, 17th April, 2012. The event will be the first of its kind bringing together the Government, private & public sector, workers community, academia, consultants and media on a single platform to share information on the development of the national talent and discuss solutions to meet the challenges related to the creation of a stronger talent pool. Oman Forum has been supported by the Ministry of Civil Services, Ministry of Higher Education, Ministry of Education, Petroleum Development Oman (PDO), Omran, AhliBank, Voltamp, The Research Council and Sultan Qaboos University.

The primary objective of Oman Forum is to promote thought leadership by disseminating information on the subject and constructing innovative ideas for a wider participation of the Omani youth in the national building process and upliftment of the society. As a valued stakeholder in the social and economic progress of the Sultanate, it is a perfect opportunity for you to attend Oman Forum and understand the best possible practices for the growth of Omani human resources for a better tomorrow. It will also be an ideal platform to network with the Ministers, senior Government officials, head honchos from the private sector, HR professionals, economists, students, business analysts, journalists and other relevant members of the society who are going to attend the discussion.

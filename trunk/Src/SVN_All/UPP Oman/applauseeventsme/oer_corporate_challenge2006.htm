The Mission of the OER Corporate Challenge is to promote health, wellness and camaraderie by presenting participants with endeavours designed to challenge their bodies and minds through the spirit of corporate competition

The Vision of the program is potentially to be Oman’s finest networking event. It will bring together every top decision maker from the corporate sector along with his key company representative’s captive for two days of experiential sessions, networking and relationship building in a relaxed and fun filled environment

Objective:

  • Creating a strategy to provide the platform for corporate teams to unwind and be motivated

  • Improved communication across businesses in Oman

  • Business Networking

  • Thinking out of the box and the most important element – HAVING FUN.

Value for sponsors:

  • Opportunity to be associated with Oman’s finest business event, being done under the flagship of the No.1 Business Magazine- Oman Economic Review

  • Networking opportunities in a very relevant ambience

  • Captive audience comprising of a cross section of the business community

  • Intensive Media/PR campaign

  • Community event – positive image


The Event:
30 teams from various local companies, in front of an audience of 1000 people over 2 days, will compete against each other in a specially designed program which will include highly interactive outdoor and indoor events that promote creative group problem solving, will encourage better bonding of the core participants and audience, eventually bringing to light the CHAMPION ORGANISATION IN OMAN.

The participants will be involved in healthy competition, will perform and demonstrate leadership, exhibit team spirit, all this while having loads of fun.

Date: 29TH and 30th Nov 2006; Venue: Oman Dive Centre

Entertainment: While the teams sweat it out during the day, the 2 evenings will be relaxing and a great networking opportunity – see Arabian theme night & Awards ceremony.

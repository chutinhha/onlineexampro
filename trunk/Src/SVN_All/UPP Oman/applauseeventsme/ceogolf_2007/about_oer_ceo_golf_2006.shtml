About OER CEO Golf 2006

OER CEO Golf 2006, Oman’s most prestigious business golfing plus networking event, is back again.

Tee off with top minds of Corporate Oman on Thursday, 23 March 2006 at the stunning Al Maha Golf Course for an action-packed 9-hole tourney. Or get introduced to the addictive pastime of golf with an exciting Golf Clinic session conducted by a pro golfer.

Sundown will see the dawn of a new exciting golfing concept in Oman – Glow Golf. A relaxed game – especially for couples – will be played along the dream like expanse of a candlelit fairway. To be followed by scrumptious dinner, dazzling entertainment, and a grand awards ceremony honouring Oman’s ace corporate golfers.

Entry to Oman’s finest corporate golfing extravaganza is by invitation only. See you there on the 23rd of March.

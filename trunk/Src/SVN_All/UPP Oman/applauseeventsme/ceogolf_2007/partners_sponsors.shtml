DaimlerChrysler

DaimlerChrysler’s passenger car brands include Maybach, Mercedes-Benz, Chrysler, Jeep®, Dodge and smart. DaimlerChrysler’s strategy rests on four pillars: global presence, superior products, leading brands, and innovation / technology leadership. DaimlerChrysler has a global workforce and a global shareholder base. With 384,723 employees, DaimlerChrysler achieved revenues of EUR 142.1 billion (USD 192.3 billion) in 2004. Muscat Golf Course Project
The Muscat Golf Course Project is Oman’s first international 18-hole green course and residential development project. Located close to Muscat's Seeb International Airport, this prestigious project is scheduled for completion in September 2006 and will create the Muscat Golf & Country Club - a five-star leisure destination comprising of a PGA championship standard golf course, an exclusive range of residential villas and apartments, a five-star destination hotel, spa facilities and a complimentary shopping centre. The Muscat Golf Course Project is endorsed by the Ministry of Tourism, with the aim of attracting international tourism and investment into Oman.

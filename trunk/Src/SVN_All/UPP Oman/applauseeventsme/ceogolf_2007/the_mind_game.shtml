The Business Golf Experience

It is said that there are two kinds of people in the corporate world: those who play Golf and those want to.

Business Golfing is one of the world’s leading corporate pastimes. It is estimated that in the U.S. alone, more than $28 billion in annual sales result directly from contacts, conversations, and relationships initiated or conducted while learning or playing golf.

Business Golfing is a high-profile mental game where the world’s top most CEOs and corporate honchos play a round of golf while making strategic moves that make or break business fortunes.

Exclusive Business Golfing events such as OER CEO Golf 2006 are ideal venues where new doors open or deals are closed. High-profile networking bees meet and connect with prospective clients and business associates on the greens. They share valuable business intelligence and insights while showing-off their big swings and power drives.

Golf is an effective tool for team building and networking as it provides a shared experience between the participants. A four to five hour round of Golf with a client or associate strengthens a relationship, and that can be the way forward for your business.

Golf is an effective way to create and maintain relationships – relationships that can improve your business. OER CEO Golf 2006 will enable Corporate Oman’s top drivers to sharpen both their business and golf skills.

Business golf is not just for "expert" golfers. It is a common misconception that only competent and experienced golfers can use golf for business advantage. While golfers who know how to play the game fairly well practice the majority of business golf, there has been a trend towards all levels of players conducting business on the course.

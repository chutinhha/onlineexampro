ALAM AL-IKTISAAD WAL A'MAL AWARDS FOR OMAN’S BEST PERFORMING COMPANIES

Alam al-Iktisaad Wal A’mal (AIWA), Oman’s leading Arabic monthly business magazine, brings to light new insights and forecasts on future trends that impact Oman’s corporate world, making it a valuable source for decision making and an indispensable tool for advertisers and marketers in Oman.

EVENT OVERVIEW
To recognize the top achievers in Oman’s corporate world, AIWA has instituted Awards for Oman's Best Performing Companies. In a red carpet awards evening at Shangri-La Barr Al Jissah on 27 June this year, AIWA will felicitate the top performing listed companies in Oman during 2010.

A networker’s dream, the event will be attended by the who’s who of Muscat corporate high life. The event will attract more than 250 business leaders, top managers and senior government officials from a cross-section of domains.

OMAN’S BEST PERFORMING COMPANIES
Overall 15 Top Ranking Companies will receive the coveted trophy. There will be five winners in each of the following three categories:

- Large-Cap (market-cap above RO75 million)
- Mid-Cap (market-cap between RO25 million to RO75 million)
- Small-Cap (market cap between RO10 million to RO25 million)


The winners have been selected based on their performance on the five key financial parameters including Revenue Growth, Net Profit Growth, Return on Average Assets, Return on Average Equity and Net Profit Margin. The ranking process is done by Gulf Baader Capital Markets (GBCM). The ranking results are validated by KPMG.

EXCELLENCE IN CORPORATE LEADERSHIP
AIWA will also felicitate two corporates with ‘Award for Excellence in Corporate Leadership’ for not only reaching the pinnacle of corporate success but also making an immense contribution in the social upliftment with their unparalleled actions.

PERSONALITY AWARDS
Alam al-Iktisaad Wal A’mal will also felicitate two prominent Omani nationals who have done the nation proud by excelling in the fields of business and economy. The awards will be presented in the following two categories:

  • GLOBAL OMANI OF THE YEAR AWARD
    The winner of ‘AIWA Global Omani of the Year Award’ is a prominent Omani who has done the Sultanate proud by taking the Omani flag to international shores with distinction, an achievement attained by a few. The winner is a person who has excelled despite odds and mitigated all risks with his courage of conviction. An epitome of exemplary leadership, he has steered his enterprise to a fast track sustainable growth path in the international market to walk away with the coveted trophy.
     

  • LIFETIME ACHIEVEMENT AWARD
    An outstanding business leadership track-record. A visionary who has changed the fortune of thousands, if not more. A person who is a benchmark for professional excellence. Someone who has all these three achievements has been the winner of AIWA’s ‘Lifetime Achievement Award’. The awardee is a widely respected and recognized businessperson or a professional who has rendered an unparalleled service, for decades, in the development of the business and economy in the Sultanate.

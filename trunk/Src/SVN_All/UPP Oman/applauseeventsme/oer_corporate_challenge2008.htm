The mission of OER Corporate Challenge is to promote health, wellness and camaraderie by presenting participants with endeavours designed to challenge their bodies and minds through the spirit of corporate competition.

The vision of the program is potentially to be Oman’s finest networking event. It will bring together every top decision maker from the corporate sector along with his key company representative’s captive for two days of experiential sessions, networking and relationship building in a relaxed and fun filled environment.

Objective:

  • Creating a strategy to provide the platform for corporate teams to unwind and be motivated

  • Improved communication across businesses in Oman

  • Business Networking

  • Thinking out of the box and the most important element – HAVING FUN

Value for sponsors:

  • Opportunity to be associated with Oman’s finest business event, being done under the flagship of the No.1 Business Magazine- Oman Economic Review

  • Networking opportunities in a very relevant ambience

  • Captive audience comprising of a cross section of the business community

  • Intensive Media/PR campaign

  • Community event – positive image


The Event:

Heart pounding, brain teasing, adrenalin pumping high-octane activities keep the 30 participating teams busy and the audience at the edge of their seats for two full days. The highly interactive outdoor and indoor activities challenge the participants both physically and mentally. The games aim to bring out leadership and teambuilding abilities in the participants. On the whole OER Corporate Challenge is an event that acts as a business breeding ground and provides a networking platform for the corporate houses in Oman.

Date: 5 Nov & 6 Nov 2008; Venue: Civil Aviation Club

Entertainment: While the teams sweat it out during the day, the 2 evenings will be relaxing and will offer an exciting entertainment.

ABOUT OER CEO GOLF

Since its conception in 2005, OER CEO Golf has been a pioneering initiative from Oman Economic Review and United Media Services, its publisher. The level of participation seen in OER CEO Golf, which witnesses an elite gathering of industry leaders coming together for the Sultanate’s biggest networking event and golf experience only goes on to show the importance of this event in Oman’s corporate calendar.

While previously, the Sultanate had only brown golf courses to boast of, today the country has a number of full fledged 18-hole PGA green golf courses designed by world renowned golfers. The 9th Edition of OER CEO Golf will be held on one such golf course, the Greg Norman-designed Almouj Golf in The Wave Muscat.

OER CEO Golf 2013 offers revitalisation for Oman’s corporate heavy weights, giving them an opportunity to network and compete on the greens. There are few events of this nature that can boast of such variety and all-round entertainment.

While out on the greens, the itinerary will include a game of golf and high powered business networking, the evening’s programme highlights include a gala dinner, world-class entertainment, socialising prize distribution, and much more.

OER CEO Golf 2013 is presented by Nawras Business Solution in association with Cadillac from OTE Group, SAMSUNG , Muriya & Times of Oman.Rolex from Khimji’s Watches is the official time keeper.

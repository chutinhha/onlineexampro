Business Meets Golf
                                                                                                      

Overview of the 9th Edition of OER CEO Golf:

OER CEO Golf 2013, the biggest event in the Sultanate’s corporate calendar, broke new ground in terms of participation, unique game format, thrilling activities and exciting prizes. Representing virtually the who’s who of Oman, the guest list comprising CEO's, VP's, managers, businessmen and their spouses.

Over 400 took part in 8th edition of OER CEO Golf held on 12th of January 2012 at the Muscat Hills Golf and Country Club.The golfers were divided into teams of four and played the 18-hole Texas Scramble. One of the most noteworthy parts of 2012 OER CEO Golf was the enthusiastic participation of a number of women golfers.

For the non-golfers, a special golfing clinic was arranged. A simple competition of chipping, pitching and putting was organised which not only enabled participants to understand the finer nuances of golf but also gave them a chance to win the biggest prize of the day – a Rolex watch from Khimji’s Watches.

On it's 9th edition also the gaming format will be same but to be played on new golf course "Almouj Golf, The Wave Muscat".

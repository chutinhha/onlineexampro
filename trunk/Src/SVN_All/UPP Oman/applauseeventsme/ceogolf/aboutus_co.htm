OER CEO Golf 2008
                                                                                             

The 5th edition of Oman’s premium golfing and networking event - OER CEO Golf 2009 - is ready to tee off at the Ghalla Wentworth Golf Club on Thursday, 12th February 2009.

Since its conception in 2005, OER CEO Golf has been a pioneering initiative from Oman Economic Review aimed at promoting Golf in Oman. The success of OER CEO Golf 2005, 2006, 2007 & 2008  has always witnessed an elite gathering of industry leaders come together for the Sultanate’s biggest networking event and golf experience

It’s a setting reserved exclusively for the captains of industry to network. If you are a golfer, you can do it over a round of golf in the 18-hole Stableford tournament. Or if you are a non-golfer you could take your first shot on the turf at the 5 hole exclusive golf round. Invite your companion to join you for the evening where fun and technology takes the front seat in as in OER CEO Golf 2009 you will witness more excitement and more enthusiasm with the first time ever appearance in Oman for the “Virtual Indoor Golf Simulator”. Surely, golfing has never been more entertaining!

And that’s not all. Look out for more excitement as the grand awards ceremony, live entertainment and gala dinner add the touches to create a spectacular finale.

Entry to Oman’s finest corporate golfing extravaganza is by invitation only. See you there on the 12th of February.

OER CEO Golf 2009 is brought you by Applause Events, the leading events specialists in Oman (A division of UMS).

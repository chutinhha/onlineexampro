  Water Golf

  
 

OER CEO Golf 2010 takes a look at the lighter side of golf with the debut in Oman of Water Golf – where everybody's takes a swing at the target on a floating 'green' set up on a water-body on the course.

As their companions join the captains of corporate Oman to play this novelty game, it sets the tone for what follows when the highpoint of the evening unfurls.


OER CEO Golf 2010 truly, brings together all the ingredients for an unforgettable occasion!

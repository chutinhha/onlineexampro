OER CEO Golf 2007 comes to a grand end
The third edition of OER CEO Golf 2007 held at the Ghalla Wentworth Golf Club on March 8, 2007, drawing an enthusiastic response from the crème de la crème of corporate Oman, ended with a resounding success. The event was a resounding memorable success both in terms of the profile of attendees, the line up of activities and prizes for participants.

Well over 200 Captains of corporate Oman, together with high profile government dignitaries came together for a relaxed day out on the fairways. The packed itinerary at OER CEO Golf 2007 was a unique blend of business and golf, catering to experienced players, amateur golfers and beginners alike.

At 1.30 pm, Dr. Amer Rawas, Managing Director of Oman Mobile and Mr Hani Al Zubair, Managing Director of Zubair Automotive llc, participated in the ceremonial tee-off to officially declare open OPER CEO GOLF 2007.

Over 70 golfers then teed off instyle for the Champions trophy and Champions Rolex to a signal which echoed amongst the dunes from the Dodge Durango awaiting the player who could shoot a hole-in-one. The golfers played in either an 18-hole Stable Ford format or a 9-hole Stable Ford Format tournament. An exciting highlight on the Course was Hole No. 2, where Justin Barnett, General Manager, Zubair Automotive awaited each golfer to a challenge of golfing skills, a challenge readily accepted by all. Exciting spot prizes sponsored by Salam Studio and Store were won by 32 golfers for the “Longest Drive” and “Closet to the Pin”.

Golfers-to-be meanwhile joined professionals, Brandon d’Souza from Tiger Sports Marketing and Xavier Bonnery and Paul Sharples from the Ghalla Wentworth Golf Club for their induction into the games finer points at the exclusive golf clinic. Topping off the day time events was the skills competition for the non golfers at the clinic.

There were no “Golf Widows” at OER CEO Golf, the organisers made sure that the highlight of the day included the golfers spouses, who joined the golfer and non-golfers as dusk was setting for what was undoubtedly an unforgettable debut of “Crazy Golf”, where couples played a round of golf to test their spirits and skills at the mini golf arena. The continuation of Glow Golf, a highly anticipated item on the itinerary saw the fairways lit up with candles, glow in the dark golf balls for couples to enjoy a relaxed two hole round.

“In view of the wonderful response to the event for a consecutive third year, OER CEO Golf has established itself as a must-attend fixture in the business and social calendar in Oman” said Alpana Roy, Asst. General Manager, United Media Services. “for months now, the corporate world was buzzing with anticipation and I believe that Thursday was an appropriate climax to the expectations of the corporate community and the hard work put in by organisers, Applause events”.

The spectacular days activities culminated in an extravagant evening of live music, a gala dinner, and an award ceremony that saw many business leaders take home glamorous prizes.

Oman's most prominent and anticipated networking-cum- Golf Tournament, has proven itself as a unique niche platform onto which the corporate decision makers congregate at the golf course amidst the spirit of competition and camraderie. As Bennet Stephens, Gulf Air's Area Manager, and one of the participants of the event said, "Events like this are very much needed on the corporate calendar to not only re-energise ourselves but for the relationships that are strengthened based on a shared experience – Golf and the phenomenal entertainment values built into the day."

Thanking the golfers, sponsors, and the Applause Events team for their support to make the event a big success, Sandeep Sehgal, General Manager, United Media Services said, "Event like OER CEO Golf are a part of our continued drive, as OER, Oman’s leading business magazine to present novel opportunities to the corporate community to come together, enjoy golf and share in the fun and excitement with their peers."

On the occasion, the chief guest His Highness Sayyid Tariq bin Shabib, Editor-in-Chief of Oman Economic Review presented the champion’s trophy and Rolex watch to Sumant Sunder, the Nett Winner of the event, and Bose home theatre system from OHI Electronics to Abdullah Mohammad, the Gross Winner.

AIWA AWARDS FOR OMAN’S BEST PERFORMING COMPANIES 

Alam al-Iktisaad Wal A’mal (AIWA), Oman’s leading Arabic monthly business magazine, brings to light new insights and forecasts on future trends that impact Oman’s corporate world, making it a valuable source for decision making and an indispensable tool for advertisers and marketers in Oman.

EVENT OVERVIEW
To recognize the top achievers in Oman’s corporate world, AIWA has instituted Awards for Oman's Best Performing Companies. In a red carpet awards evening at Shangri-La Barr Al Jissah on 10 June this year, AIWA felicitated the top performing listed companies in Oman during 2011.

A networker’s dream, the event was attended by the who’s who of Muscat corporate high life. The event attracted more than 250 business leaders, top managers and senior government officials from a cross-section of domains.

OMAN’S BEST PERFORMING COMPANIES
Overall 15 Top Ranking Companies received the coveted trophy. The top five rankings in each category took into account the defined set of broader parameters including:

Growth
Revenue Growth Year on Year Growth (FY11)
Operating Profit (EBIT) Growth Year on Year Growth (FY11)
Net Profit Growth Year on Year Growth (FY11)
Efficiency
Return on Average Assets (RoAA) Return calculated on average assets (FY11)
Return on Average Equity (RoAE) Return calculated on average equity (FY11)
Operating Profit Margin (EBIT) Productivity ratio (FY11)
Net Profit Margin (NPM) Profitability ratio (FY11)

The ranking process was done by Gulf Baader Capital Markets (GBCM). The ranking results were validated by KPMG.

BEST ENGINEERING CONSULTANT


This will be awarded to a consultant who has managed prestigious projects in terms of scale, technical innovation, business performance and overall contribution to Oman’s construction and infrastructure industry. Winning entry would have demonstrably placed the client’s needs at the heart of the project and then delivered better bottom-line performance, high customer retention, profitability and positive reputation as well as clear strategies that led to sustained improvement.

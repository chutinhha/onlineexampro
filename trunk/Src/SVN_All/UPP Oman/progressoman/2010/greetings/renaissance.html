Every now and then in history, an exceptional leader emerges who proves to be the catalyst for a quantum leap in a nation’s progress and development. Forty years ago, the 23rd of July 1970 proved to be a day of destiny for the Sultanate of Oman with the advent of the reign of His Majesty Sultan Qaboos bin Said. From that day, Oman has been blessed with a leader of vision, courage and purpose. His Majesty restored Oman’s sense of nationhood and swept in unprecedented progress to create a vibrant modern economy, whist preserving all that is precious of Oman’s rich heritage and culture. His Majesty trusted his fellow countrymen. He knew instinctively that, given opportunity and access to resources, the Omani people would deliver the rebirth of a great nation. This year we all join hands to celebrate 40 years of Oman’s modern renaissance.

Mr. Stephen R. Thomas OBE

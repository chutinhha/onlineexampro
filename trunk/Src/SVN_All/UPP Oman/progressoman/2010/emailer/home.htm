In another first United Media Services has launched the digital version of Progress 2010-2011, its annual publication which celebrates the Sultanate’s Renaissance Day. The digital version can be viewed on -- www.progressoman.com

The comprehensive ‘Progress 2010-2011’ (www.progressoman.com) provides readers with easy online access to all the pages of the bilingual publication, making it accessible via all acknowledged internet browsers without additional software installation.

The online edition will also take the readers to the index pages of each advertiser in the publication with a hyperlink taking them to their websites. Being the first annual publication in the country to go online, ‘Progress’ digital provides a wonderful opportunity for advertisers to connect with business travellers, local residents and an international audience. Besides, a separate link is given to view the greeting messages and video clippings of various industry heads on the occasion of the 40th anniversary of the Renaissance.

Readers can also download the PDF version of each article in the latest issue of ‘Progress’ which stands out for its exhaustive coverage of various sectors such as Oil and Gas, Banking and Finance, Capital Markets, Education and Healthcare, Insurance, Agriculture and Poultry, Retailing, Aviation, Information Technology, Real estate, Telecom, Manufacturing and Logistics, Power, Infrastructure, Tourism and Trade. Each of these sectors is covered in dedicated chapters highlighting the strides taken by the Sultanate in these areas.

Progress digital is a path breaking move in Oman’s publishing history as it offers convenience, relevant content and a world of opportunity for companies to connect with their relevant target audience.

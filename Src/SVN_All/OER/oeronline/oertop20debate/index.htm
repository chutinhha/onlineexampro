Oman Economic Review (OER) in Association with the Capital Market Authority (CMA) is organizing a first of its kind OER Top 20 Debate & Awards Ceremony on the State of Sultanate’s Economy. The seminar will see the participation of a cross section of CEO’s from various sectors, who will speak about the challenges and opportunities facing their respective sectors and companies. The deliberations of this unique event will help CEO’s and senior executives to gain valuable insights into tackling the critical issues arising from the global financial meltdown.
 
Date: 31st May 2009, 4:00p.m -7:00 pm
Venue: Capital Market Authority Auditorium
Dress Code: National/Formal

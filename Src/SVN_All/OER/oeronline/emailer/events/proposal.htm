Oman Green Awards
5th June 2011


Sub: Proposal to Partner with Oman Green Awards 2011


Firstly, it is my pleasure to brief you about Oman Green Awards, as the country’s first Environmental Awards, the Oman Green Awards have been created to awaken the eco-consciousness in every citizen and resident of Oman. The ultimate aim is to motivate behavioral change and increase awareness in relation to the protection and preservation of our environment.

Born out of the unwavering belief that our Earth is a shared resource and therefore a shared responsibility, these awards will recognise and promote voluntary contributions across a broad spectrum of activities both from individuals and companies.

With the full support of Ministry of Environment and Climate Change, Ministry of Health, Muscat municipality and Environment Society of Oman, the first edition of Oman Green Awards was held on the 12th of June 2010 alongside the world environment day at Shangri-La Barr Al Jissah resort & Spa as a platform to honor and appreciate such outstanding environmental vision, endeavors, initiatives and achievements.

For the 2011 edition of the Oman Green Awards we take this opportunity to invite you to become a part of this national initiative and become a Green Oman Partner.

As a Green Oman Partner you will have the following benefits:

  • Company will be showcased throughout the initiative as ‘Green Oman Partner’ -Inspiring a Better Tomorrow; Committed to Keeping Oman Green

  • Logo presence in all advertisements and PR for a one year period starting MAR 2011 ( Newspaper & Magazines )

  • One award presentation by a senior member of the company

  • Four VIP invites for the awards function

  • Acknowledgement by the MC

  • Branding visibility on the website www.omangreenawards.com with a hyperlink to the company site

  • Your Green initiative’s showcased on the OGA website

  • One editorial feature of two pages in OER and Alam Aliktisaad Wala’mal, focusing on your company’s initiatives and activities, during the year. Or a relevant editorial can be featured in line with your objectives

  • You will be acknowledged in all future endeavors under the OGA platform for a one-year period

  • Branding Visibility through logo presence at the event on backdrop and other event related collaterals

  • Opportunity to network with the heads of companies/invitees during the awards function

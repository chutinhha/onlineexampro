                                                                                                                                     5 May, 2012

After Compliments,

 

On the 10th of June 2012, Oman’s Best Performing Companies are going to be felicitated amongst an august gathering of the Who’s who of the corporate world. The AIWA AWARDS FOR BEST PERFORMING COMPANIES are instituted by Alam al-Iktisaad Wal A’mal (AIWA), Oman’s premier business magazine published by United Media Services LLC.

In a red carpet awards evening at Shangri-La's Barr Al Jissah Resort & Spa, AIWA will felicitate the top performing listed companies in Oman during 2011. A networker’s dream, this premium event, with dinner, will attract more than 250 top business leaders along with their spouses; CEOs and senior government officials from a cross-section of industries, ministries and government bodies. It will witness the congregation of the key decision-makers who are driving the country’s business and economy. A high-voltage entertainment show will make the attendees cherish the evening for a long time to come.

We invite your organization to celebrate the success of the best performers in Oman by reserving a table at the high-profile event. The brief details of AIWA AWARDS FOR BEST PERFORMING COMPANIES and the seat reservations are as follows:

DATE: 10 June (Sunday), 2012
   
VENUE: Shangri-La's Barr Al Jissah Resort & Spa
   
TIMING: 7 pm onwards
   
SEAT RESERVATIONS: Table booking (seating for 8 persons) – RO800.000 per table
  Seat booking –RO125.000 per seat
   
   

Overall 15 Top Ranking Companies will receive the coveted Best Performing Company trophy. AIWA will also felicitate a company for its immense contribution to the society and economy. It will be presented with the trophy for ‘Excellence in Corporate Leadership’. Two prominent Omani nationals who have done the nation proud by excelling in the field of business and economy will be given ‘AIWA Global Omani of the Year Award’ and ‘AIWA Lifetime Achievement Award’. Gulf Baader Capital Markets SAOC (GBCM) is the knowledge partner whereas KPMG is the validation partner for the ranking process used for the awards.

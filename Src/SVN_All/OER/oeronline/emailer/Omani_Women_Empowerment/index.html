Omani Women’s Empowerment project
The Art of Living Organization is organizing an Omani Women’s Empowerment project, which will be hosted by the Art of Living Organization in collaboration with the Matrix Business Academy. The aim of this event would be to empower Young Omani Women who desire to start their own home run business.

The Matrix Business Academy will run a 10 hours workshop over five days, guiding the participants on methods to begin and sustain their business. These participants will then be mentored for three months after the program by the Matrix Academy to start and sustain their business.

Alongside, these handpicked lady entrepreneurs in making, will be taught techniques of breathing, yoga and meditation. This workshop will not only remove stress from the participants, but will also improve their focus, concentration and intuition. These tools and methods imparted during the Art of Living program will help these participants on becoming more dynamic and enthusiastic entrepreneurs.

OCCI, in this project of nation building and increasing the human capacities of Omani Women is supporting by providing its premises for the workshop.

 Workshop Details
September 22 - 26, 2013
Time : 8.30 am to 1 pm

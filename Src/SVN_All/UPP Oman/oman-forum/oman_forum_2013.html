OMAN FORUM 2014: JOB CREATION - THE NATIONAL AGENDA
Held under the auspices of HE Darwish Al Balushi, Minister of Financial Affairs, Oman Forum 2013 led to discussions on the solutions for maintaining a balance between job creation and business growth as well as promotion of small and medium enterprises by the relevant stakeholders from all walks of the society including the Government, private sector, HR experts and civil society.
Panel 1: Job Creation - Maintaining a Balance Between the Social Obligation and Business Growth
  • HE Mohsin al Balushi, Advisor, Ministry of Commerce & Industry
  • Ahmed Al Wahaibi, CEO, Oman Oil Co.
  • Salim Naseer Al- Hadhrami, Director General - Planning and Development, Ministry of Manpower
  • Hussain Salman al Lawati, MD, Oman Cables Industry
  • Eng. Saleh Al Shanfari, Chairman, A’Saffa Foods
  • Said Al Hamdani, AGM & Head-HR, Ahli Bank
Panel 2: SMEs - Time To Play Their Due Role in Job Creation
  • Mundhir al Barwani, Director-External Affairs, Petroleum Development Oman
  • Salah Al Mawali, DG-SME, Ministry of Commerce & Industry
  • Abdullah Al Jufaili, GM, Sharakah
  • Hussain Ghuloom, CEO, Al Waqia Shoes
  • Ilham Al Hamid, AGM-SME, BankMuscat
  • Hassan M. Juma, MD, Mohammed Juma Sultan Co.
Programme Focus

Divided into two panel discussions, Oman Forum discussed the following:
Session I
Job Creation - Maintaining a Balance Between the Social Obligation and Business Growth

Panel discussion on the current status of development of national talent, gap between the demand and supply of talent, and the associated challenges. The panelists will focus on :

  • Interests of private sector being comprised after the social unrest
  • Impact of recent amendments in labour laws on the business expansion & new investments
  • Creation of jobs - Whose job is it?
  • The Government caught up between meeting public expectations and managing private sector growth simultaneously.
  • How to draw a line between social responsibility and business efficiency & profit orientation?
Session II
SMEs - Time To Play Their Due Role in Job Creation

Panel discussion on what are the possible solutions to meet the talent gap and develop Omani talent on a sustainable basis to support the national growth engine. The panel will debate on:
  • Have SMEs been accorded their due status in the overall economic eco-system?
  • How SMEs could relieve the nation from the burden of job creation?
  • In which sectors, SMEs could create large number of jobs?
  • What’s stopping young Omanis from becoming entrepreneurs in true sense in large numbers?

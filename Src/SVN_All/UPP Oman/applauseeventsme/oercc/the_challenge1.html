Physical Games:

Five-A-Side Soccer
An extraordinary team sport that teaches you vital skills for success in the corporate world. A sublime game that tests leadership, talent and teamwork, and also keeps everyone entertained. Tip: The right game plan wins you the game.

Beach Volleyball
A sport that reminds you that you can still beat competition by having fun! Soak in the sun and sprawl in the sand, but don’t forget to smack your competition hard. Corporate Insight: Work hard, play harder.

Relay Race
It’s a different kind of rat race. On your marks… get set… go!!! But do pass on the baton to the next runner – a team becomes successful only when everyone in the chain of command does their job well – a key lesson in the corporate world.

Tug of War
Every business has only one strategic focus – success. Whether you are at the rope’s end or middle, you have only one goal – to win. Just the way it’s in business.

Camp Up
Build a tent from ground zero, while the clock is ticking. The fastest team to set up camp wins. In hindsight: Talent wins matches. Teamwork wins championships.

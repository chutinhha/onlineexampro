OER Corporate Challenge Ignites Muscat’s Competitive Spirit
Times of Oman - October 9, 2006

Muscat: The corporate world in Muscat is gearing itself up for an exciting challenge – one that will test the sheer grit and guts of its people. The OER Corporate Challenge will aim at promoting health, wellness and camaraderie by presenting participants with games designed to challenge their bodies and minds through the spirit of corporate competition. The vision of the program is to bring together top decision makers from the corporate sector along with the key company representatives for two days of networking and relationship building in a relaxed and fun filled environment.

To be held at the Oman Dive Center on 29th and 30th of November, the two day event is set to attract over 1000 spectators. 30 leading business houses and corporations in Muscat are expected to send in teams – each comprising 8 members including at least one lady participant.

The OER Corporate Challenge has already sparked the imagination of business managers and leaders. It is a pioneering effort aimed at bring the corporate world together in more ways than one. There is a buzz of excitement as corporate houses are choosing their best and most able representatives for the upcoming challenge. Teams are eagerly awaiting the opening of registration and have begun training in preparation of the forthcoming battle of wits and the ensuing prizes up for grabs.

The OER Corporate Challenge is OER’s formula for building leadership, teamwork, communication and strategic planning. Participating teams will be taxed on their levels of creativity, dexterity and strength. Key points to keep in mind during the OER Corporate Challenge will be to prepare a clear plan prior for the challenge at hand, strengthen communication with everyone on the team, the ability to execute individual jobs, everyone working together towards a common goal, execute the plan and finally, to have fun at your job.

 AIWA Awards for Best Performing Companies

To recognize the top achievers in Oman’s corporate world, Alam
al-Iktisaad Wal A’mal (AIWA) has instituted annual AIWA Awards for Oman's Best Performing Companies. Alam al-Iktisaad Wal A’mal (AIWA), published by United Media Services, is Oman’s leading fortnightly Arabic business magazine.

Every year, a joint AIWA-Gulf Baader Capital Markets (GBCM) survey identifies the best performing companies on select seven financial parameters in three segments - large cap, mid cap and small cap. The
companies listed on Muscat Securities Market (MSM) are taken into
consideration for the ranking process. The results are validate by
KPMG. A detailed report on the survey is published in a special
edition of AIWA magazine.

 The top ranked companies are presented with a glittering AIWA trophy in a red carpet awards evening. In addition, select number of prominent management heads and leading organizations are also
recognized for their remarkable achievements and contribution to the
economy and society. The event is attended by the who’s who of Muscat corporate high life. The premium event attracts more than 300 top business leaders, CEOs and senior government officials from a cross-section of industries, ministries and government bodies.

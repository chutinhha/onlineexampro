ALAM AL-IKTISAAD WAL A'MAL AWARDS
Oman’s Best Performing Companies

 

Alam al-Iktisaad Wal A’mal (AIWA), Oman’s leading Arabic monthly business magazine, brings to light new insights and forecasts on future trends that impact Oman’s corporate world, making it a valuable source for decision making and an indispensable tool for advertisers and marketers in Oman.

EVENT OVERVIEW
To recognize the top achievers in Oman’s corporate world, AIWA has instituted Awards for Oman's Best Performing Companies. In a red carpet awards evening at Al Bustan Palace on 30 May this year, AIWA will felicitate the top performing listed companies in Oman during 2010. A networker’s dream, the event will be attended by the who’s who of Muscat corporate high life. The event will attract more than 200 business leaders, top managers and senior government officials from a cross-section of domains.

OMAN’S BEST PERFORMING COMPANIES
Overall 15 Top Ranking Companies will receive the coveted trophy. There will be five winners in each of the following three categories:
- Large-Cap (market-cap above RO75 million)
- Mid-Cap (market-cap between RO25 million to RO75 million)
- Small-Cap (market cap between RO10 million to RO25 million)

The winners will be selected based on their performance on the five key financial parameters including Revenue Growth, Net Profit Growth, Return on Average Assets, Return on Average Equity and Net Profit Margin. The ranking process will be done by Gulf Baader Capital Markets (GBCM).

PERSONALITY AWARDS
Alam al-Iktisaad Wal A’mal will also felicitate two prominent Omani nationals who have done the nation proud by excelling in the fields of business and economy. The personalities will be selected by the editorial board of AIWA based on pre-defined criteria summarized below. The awards will be presented in the following two categories:

GLOBAL OMANI OF THE YEAR AWARD
The winner of ‘AIWA Global Omani of the Year Award’ will be a prominent Omani who has done the Sultanate proud by taking the Omani flag to international shores with distinction, an achievement attained by a few. The winner will be a person who has excelled despite odds and mitigated all risks with his courage of conviction. An epitome of exemplary leadership, the person who has steered his enterprise to a fast track sustainable growth path in the international market will walk away with the coveted trophy.

LIFE TIME ACHIEVEMENT AWARD
An outstanding business leadership track-record. A visionary who has changed the fortune of thousands, if not more. A person who is a benchmark for professional excellence. Someone who has all these three achievements can only aspire to be the winner of AIWA’s ‘Lifetime Achievement Award’. The awardee will be a widely respected and recognized businessperson or a professional who has rendered an unparalleled service, for decades, in the development of the business and economy in the Sultanate.

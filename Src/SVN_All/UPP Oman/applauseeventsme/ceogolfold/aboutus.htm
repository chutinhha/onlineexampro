The third edition of Oman’s premium golfing and networking event - OER CEO Golf 2007 - is ready to tee off at the Ghallah Wentworth Golf Club on Thursday, 8 March 2007.

It’s a setting reserved exclusively for the captains of industry to network. If you are a golfer, you can do it over a round of golf in an 18-hole or 9-hole Stableford tournament. Or link up at the golf clinic as you pick up the finer points of golf. Invite your companion to join you for the debut of ‘Crazy Golf’ where fun takes the front seat in an out-of-the-ordinary miniature golf course. The exotic ‘Glow Golf’ returns again to Oman with its candlelit fairways and romantic ambience. Surely, golfing has never been more entertaining!

And that’s not all. Look out for more excitement as the grand awards ceremony, live entertainment and gala dinner add the touches to create a spectacular finale.

Entry to Oman’s finest corporate golfing extravaganza is by invitation only. See you there on the 8th of March.

OER CEO Golf 2007 is brought you by Applause Events, the leading events specialists in Oman, in association with Tiger Sports, the exclusive marketing partner of the Indian PGA.

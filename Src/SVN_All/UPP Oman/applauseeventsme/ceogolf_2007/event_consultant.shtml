Tiger Sports Marketing Pvt. Ltd., India
Oman Economic Review (OER) has partnered with Tiger Sports Marketing Pvt. Ltd. (TSM) from India, a leading sports management company with a primary focus on golf, to present OER CEO Golf 2006. With a wide range of operating interests in every aspect of the game, TSM is widely acknowledged today as The Golf Company in India. Tiger Sports Marketing is primarily into managing and promoting golf events, marketing the Indian PGA, Amateur and Junior Golf Tours, television production, golf media management, player management, providing consultancy to golf projects providing content for GolfStyle, the official magazine of the Indian PGA Tour. TSM holds exclusive marketing and management rights to the Professional Golfers Association's (PGA) Indian Tour.

The fourth Edition of OER CEO Golf 2009 worked wonders at creating a buzz among the business drivers of Oman. Held at the Ghallah Wentworth Club on the 21st of February 2008, it was a rare opportunity to catch the captains of the corporate world connecting over a game of Golf.

Golfers enjoyed the putts, birdies and even the bogeys. There was also room for some serious business talk over the intense 18-hole game. The aspiring Golfers got valuable tips from the pros and tested their skills on a new platform in an exclusive 4-hole round of Golf.

That’s not all; the evening was lined up with special attractions like Virtual Golf, Wheel of Fortune, and an exotic dance performance by a Columbian Troupe which made this a memorable event.

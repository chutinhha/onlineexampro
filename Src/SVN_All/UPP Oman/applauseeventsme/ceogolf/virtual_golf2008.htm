Golfsimulator - The ultimate indoor golf experience

 The GolfBlaster3 System is a turnkey solution and is built around a highly accurate ball and club tracking system, integrated with the world's most realistic golf simulation software. It is designed to provide the ultimate in functionality for PLAY, PRACTICE, SELF ANALYSIS, TEACHING and CLUBFITTING. Combine top spec technology, realistic graphics and superb special effects and enjoy the ultimate in accuracy, realism with total ease of use.

Golf All Year Round! The simulator enables the golfer to enjoy the game around the clock, regardless of the weather or the time of day. But the weather conditions significantly influence the parameters of the shot, so you can set create diverse virtual weather, course and game conditions to further enhance your game. Operating the simulator is simple – just push the start button; the simulator does not require operating staff and/or personnel. During the game only minimal interaction is required between player and console. To make life even easier the console has a touch screen with symbols representing individual functions and the overall control is simplified to one touch of your finger on the corresponding symbol.

Applause Events, a part of United Media Services, is a one-of-a-kind event management specialist in the Sultanate of Oman.

From event conception, planning and coordination; logistics management; getting ideas, people and equipment in place; developing marketing communications; ensuring adequate media coverage; to all the works that make any event successful – we are specialists working with the aim to create an impact that always delivers applause. In every event. Event after event.

Applause Events brings to you high-profile events such as :

 - Oman Debate
- Alam Al Iktisaad Wal Amal Awards for Best Performing Companies
- Oman Forum
- Oman Green Awards 
- OER Top 20 Awards
- Dossier Construction Awards & Summit
- Al Mar'a Excellence Awards
- Signature Couture Show
- OER CEO Golf



Through these events, our aim is to bring together members of Oman’s corporate community on a common platform and provide them top-quality networking opportunities. Moreover, they put the spotlight on Oman’s leading brands, business people and corporate organisations.

Today, being a part of an Applause Event is an assured way to maximise your marketing reach, boost your brand equity, and enhance your corporate image.

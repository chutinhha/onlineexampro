OER CEO GOLF
March 2005 & 2006

In 2005, OER was the first to introduce Business Golfing in Oman by through OER CEO Golf, one of the most awaited events in Oman’s corporate calendar.

Presented by Oman Economic Review - Oman’s No.1 business magazine, OER CEO Golf brings together leaders and key decision makers of Corporate Oman in a high-powered event networking event that combines business golf with pleasure.

In March 2005, the inaugural edition of OER CEO Golf created history with its phenomenal response and hype generated.

The second edition of OER CEO Golf, held on the 23rd of March 2006, broke new ground when top corporate decision-makers in Oman came together at the Ghallah Wentworth Golf Course to tee-off for an exciting 15-hour golfing extravaganza.

The main tournament, a nine-hole game, commenced in the afternoon and was played on Stableford format. Over 60 corporate golfers battled it out for the ultimate crowning glory – the title of OER CEO Golf Champion for the year 2006.

A Golf Clinic for non-golfers followed the main tournament, and ended with a skills competition in four distinct disciplines of golf. It was a very memorable experience for the VIP guests who were not playing in the tournament, but still wanted a swing of golfing action as well.

The highlight of the event was the special debut game of Glow Golf for couples on a candle-lit fairway. It added a glowing touch to the evening, with its magical candlelit fairways and romantic ambience. This was followed by scrumptious dinner, dazzling entertainment, and a grand awards ceremony honouring Oman’s ace corporate golfers.

OER conceptualised and staged this event in association with Tiger Sports Marketing, the exclusive marketing arm of the Indian PGA tour.

The event received rave reviews for its superb hospitality, innovative marketing activities, attention to detail and quality of entertainment, and was attended by over 200 corporate captains and their partners.

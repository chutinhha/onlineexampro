DOSSIER PROFILE

ABOUT DOSSIER CONSTRUCTION MAGAZINE

Dossier-Construction is Oman’s first and only Business to Business (B2B) magazine on the sector for the last seven years. Published by United Media Services (UMS), known for Oman Economic Review magazine, Dossier-Construction targets the who’s who of the industry: procurement and purchasing heads, top officials in the ministries, senior-to-mid level managers in the construction firms, independent consultants and building material suppliers, industry bodies and visitors/participants in relevant trade forums and more. An authoritative source of information, Dossier-Construction is a strategic partner with all the major industry trade shows in Muscat.

EVENT CONCEPT



DOSSIER CONSTRUCTION AWARDS & SUMMIT 2013

UMS is recognized for premier corporate events such as OMAN DEBATE hosted by Tim Sebastian, OMAN FORUM hosted by Yusuf Al Hooti, OER TOP 20 AWARDS, AIWA AWARDS, OER CEO GOLF, etc. After the unprecedented success of DOSSIER-CONSTRUCTION print magazine, UMS took the opportunity to connect with the industry stakeholders through DOSSIER CONSTRUCTION AWARDS & SUMMIT 2013 -- an exclusive construction, real estate and infrastructure industry event.

The objective was to felicitate industry performers, individuals, best practices, technology and project excellence through an awards ceremony; and drive the exchange of knowledge and best practices within the industry through an intellectual forum in the form of a Summit.

The one day event included a half-day long Summit followed by a special award ceremony in the evening felicitating the best of the best within the construction, infrastructure, real estate, interiors and building materials' industries.

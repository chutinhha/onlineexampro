OER Debate 2009: State of the Economy  
     
     
     
     
 

Is this the end of free market capitalism?

Will business improve in 2010?

Do companies need to live on lower profits?
 

These were just some of the questions discussed in the OER Debate 2009. Presented by OER and the Capital Market Authority, the debate was the first of its kind in the country.

CEO’s, chairman’s, and ministers alike came together to discuss the challenges and opportunities facing companies and sectors in the wake of the global economic recession.

Moderated by Philip Stanton, Managing Partner of Ernst & Young, the debate generated avid interest with panelists and participants of the highest caliber.
